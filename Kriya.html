KRIYA-Event Management Club



Club Overview

Kriya is our university level student club for organizing all academic, co-curricular and extra-curricular events within the campus.

Time Management, Strategic Management, Resource Management, Finance Management. All these put together are trained here through the perfect Event Management.

If you want to make your CV heavier, join the club and experience life.

Activities of KRIYA

  • Organize at least 5 events per year including Alumni meet, Orientation program, Graduation Day, Annual Day etc, Training to the volunteers from event management organizations, connecting to external event management firms, increasing the scope of consultancy services through activities, Improving the institutional resources to facilitate better activities, To successfully organize events in Recruitment of new volunteers.
  • Organizing intramural competitions related to creativity, leadership skills and strategic management
