Club Overview

Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured foreverâ€¦ It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything.

Objectives of CLICKCADETS:

  • To foster a community of photography enthusiasts, from beginners to advanced levels.
  • To organize workshops and sessions on various photography techniques (e.g., portrait, landscape, macro, street photography).
  • To conduct photo walks and excursions to practice photography in diverse environments.
  • To host photography competitions and exhibitions to showcase members' talents.
  • To provide a platform for members to share feedback, learn from each other, and grow their skills.

Outcomes:

  • Members will gain a deeper understanding of photographic principles and practices.
  • Improved technical skills in camera operation, lighting, and composition.
  • Development of a personal photographic style and artistic vision.
  • Opportunities for networking with fellow photographers and industry professionals.
  • Creation of a strong portfolio of high-quality photographic work.

If you have a passion for capturing moments and telling stories through images, join Clickcadets!
